About

Our Journey

  • 2016 NOV 11

    A Journey For Unity

    AKKRRITI Club initiated with 5 core members to carry out technical and cultural activities in the campus of Sambalpur University Institute Of Information Technology as a Non-Official Student Initiative Club Of Suiit.

  • 2017 JAN 26

    When We Grown Up

    The Club is credited for oraganising SUIIT Premier League for the season 2017, a 4 day inter-college cricket tournament in the SUIIT Cricket Ground with 100% financial support and first time organised sports event in SUIIT.

  • 2017 FEB 28

    Came to Street Of Success

    Club successfully organised INOVOVATE'17 as official titled sponsor and the fest became the largest technology festival of western-odisha and the longest technology exhibition of Odisha.

  • 2017 JULY 14

    Now We Are...

    We are approved by Science Education Initiative India,Office of National President as per the ACT 1765/RULE(A) and ACT 1765/RULE 98(K).As the National techno-cultural club of India now we have a budget of 5 Lakhs 57 Thousand for the technical activities as well as the cultural activities, now we have our own self-financed Robotics Research Company,National Publication House,Charitable Trust and Student project funding society.
